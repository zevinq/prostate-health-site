《中国男性前列腺健康白皮书》2022 数据显示：

According to the "White Paper on Male Prostate Health in China" released on October 28, 2022,：

前列腺炎、前列腺增生发病超过2年，有43%的患者会引发阳痿，93%的患者会发生早泄，10%的患者几乎完全丧失男性功能！前列腺炎以18-54岁患者为主，其中18-24岁患者就诊比例显著上升。前列腺增生患者以45岁以上为主。另一项调查表明，80%以上的男性不育症都是由前列腺疾病引起。在发病10年以上的前列腺疾病患者群体中，有45%会发生癌变，世界上每年因前列腺癌死亡男性达50万人，并呈每年上升趋势。”

  • Among patients with prostatitis or prostatic hyperplasia lasting over two years:
    • 43% develop erectile dysfunction.
    • 93% experience premature ejaculation.
    • 10% almost completely lose male sexual function.
  • Prostatitis primarily affects men aged 18 to 54, with a significant increase in patients aged 18 to 24 seeking medical attention.
  • Prostatic hyperplasia predominantly affects men over the age of 45.
  • Over 80% of male infertility cases are caused by prostate-related diseases.
  • Among patients with prostate conditions lasting over 10 years, 45% are at risk of developing cancer.
  • Globally, prostate cancer causes 500,000 deaths annually, with the number increasing each year.
